Top Attractions

Dublin Castle

Historic castle in the heart of the city, with centuries of political history and impressive architecture.

Trinity College

Ireland's oldest university, famous for its beautiful campus and the illuminated manuscript Book of Kells (9th century).

St. Patrick's Cathedral

The largest cathedral in Ireland, dedicated to the country's patron saint.

Traditional Foods

Irish Stew

A comforting stew made with lamb (or beef), potatoes, carrots, onions, and herbs — the ultimate Irish classic.

Fish and Chips

Crispy battered fish (often cod or haddock) served with thick-cut fries and tartar sauce.

Irish Coffee

Coffee with Irish whiskey, sugar, and whipped cream.

Culture

Dublin's culture blends rich literary heritage, vibrant music, and warm social spirit.
